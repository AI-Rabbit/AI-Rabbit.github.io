Research Projects

2.6 GHz RIS Algorithm Design and Field Trial Validation

Key Contributor

Sep 2022 — Jun 2024

Focused on RIS deployment in commercial 5G networks to enhance coverage in weak-signal areas. Developed a two-stage spatial-oversampling codebook beamforming algorithm based on channel spatial structure, significantly reducing search complexity while maintaining excellent performance. The system achieved 4–7 dB outdoor signal gain in China Mobile's live network.

RIS-Based Anti-Jamming Communication System

Key Contributor

Sep 2019 — Dec 2022

Developed a 5.8 GHz RIS-based anti-jamming wireless communication system. As a core member, designed efficient parallel multi-channel RIS control systems and FPGA-embedded control programs for precise RIS manipulation. The system passed acceptance by a research institute at NUDT, demonstrating practical engineering value.

Massive MIMO High-Performance Transmission Theory and Technology

Key Contributor

Sep 2023 — Present

Investigating high-performance low-complexity implementations for massive MIMO using Dynamic Metasurface Antennas (DMA). Established a DMA communication model considering Lorentzian constraints and arbitrary discrete phase shifts. Proposed nearest-point projection and optimal beamforming algorithms, deriving closed-form expressions for average SNR and performance bounds, validated via Monte Carlo simulations. Submitted to a top-tier journal.
